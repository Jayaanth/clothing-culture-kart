Origin

Designed after hours. Worn outside the lines.

Clothing Culture Kart began with a simple belief: local fashion can feel raw, premium, and personal at the same time. The brand takes the discipline of engineering and puts it into drops, fits, fabric decisions, print durability, and the details that make a garment survive real street wear.

Built by Engineers

Mysuru First

Indie Label

Community Driven

Instagram-led feedback, WhatsApp orders, and styles shaped by real wearers.

Brand Statement

Wear attitude before trends.

The label exists for people who like their clothes with weight, contrast, and identity. Dark maroon, matte black, burnt orange, oversized type, and garage-culture grit are not decoration here. They are the signal.
