About the Brand

More than clothing.

Clothing Culture Kart is an independent streetwear label from Mysuru, started by engineers and built around local community, garage culture, bold typography and limited drop energy.

Designed for people who wear attitude before trends, the brand blends practical build quality with raw street identity: dark maroon, matte black, burnt orange, oversized type and clothing that feels local without looking generic.
